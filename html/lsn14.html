LESSON 14: Class 4 (continued)

Vocabulary

  • icēte - letter
  • icūma - iron, metal
  • icāha - sin
  • icōbo - hole (in ground)
  • igikōko - wild, animal, insect
  • hanze - outside, out of doors

22. These prefixes do not appear the same as those in the previous lesson, but they still belong to 4th class.

  • a) The prefix ki before a vowel becomes c: bi before a vowel becomes vy.

    e.g. iki-ete becomes icete ibi-ete becomes ivyete
    e.g. iki-aha becomes icaha ibi-aha becomes ivyaha

  • b) This same rule applies to adjectives and verbs:

    ki-izi becomes ciza bi-iza becomes vyiza
    ki-inshi becomes cinshi bi-inshi becomes vyinshi
