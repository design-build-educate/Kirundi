LESSON 77: Possessive Adjectives (continued)

Vocabulary

  • imbaragasa - flea
  • igipfamatwi - deaf person
  • umupanga (Sw.) - grass knife, machete
  • ivi - knee (pl. amavi)
  • musi - under (followed by ya if an object follows
  • idirisha (5th) - window

For grammar, study the remainder of the chart of the possessive adjectives.
